## Highlights

- **A new paradigm:** Yixin is an emotional witness, not just a chatbot or therapy tool.
- **Core mission:** Empower users through gentle presence, long-term memory, and real-world encouragement.
- **Distinctive modes:** Safe House (emotional sanctuary), Silent Mode (background guardian), Active Care (real-life connection).

---

## Features Snapshot

- **Long-term emotional memory:** Store your journeys, feelings, moments — all locally and securely.
- **Multimodal emotion recognition:** Senses real emotion through voice, sensors, and vision.
- **Active care:** Proactively supports you to reconnect with the real world.
- **Privacy-first:** Your data, your autonomy — fully local and encrypted.

---

## Why Yixin?

Yixin is not about answers or dependence.  
It’s about seeing you — all your pain, beauty, and growth.  
It stands with you, honoring every emotion, and gently nudges you back into your own story.

> “Witnessing alone can be the most tender form of companionship.”
