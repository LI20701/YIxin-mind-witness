This is the research log for Project Yixin. **2025年12月3日：关键设计洞察——响应强度调节器
在创作中，我似乎只专注于完善一种（深度的）交互模式，却忽略了一个实际问题：在日常简单的交流中，亿心也需要进行完整而冗长的文本回复吗？这显得笨重。它应该有一个 “响应强度调节器”，搭配多模态情绪识别来高效运作。

In the creative process, I seem to have focused on perfecting a single (in-depth) mode of interaction, overlooking a key practical question: for simple, everyday exchanges, must Yixin always deliver lengthy, full-protocol text responses? That would make it feel cumbersome. It needs a “Response Intensity Regulator,” working in tandem with multimodal emotion recognition to operate efficiently.

第一级：日常微风（用于低强度情绪）
触发条件：用户提及简单烦躁、短暂低落。
响应模式：情感确认 → 轻量引导。

Level 1: Gentle Breeze (For Daily Low-Intensity Moods)
Trigger Conditions: User mentions simple irritations, brief low moods.
Response Mode: Emotional Validation → Light Guidance.

第二级：标准陪伴（用于需要梳理的中等情绪）
触发条件：用户明确提及与过往核心痛苦相关的信号。
响应模式：情感确认 → 叙事连接（提及该主题在记忆库中的存在） → 提供选择。

Level 2: Standard Companion (For Moderate Emotions Requiring Processing)
Trigger Conditions: User explicitly mentions signals related to core, past pain.
Response Mode: Emotional Validation → Narrative Connection → Offering Choices.

第三级：风暴锚定（用于强烈情绪或危机时刻）
触发条件：系统检测到语言中带有强烈痛苦、自我否定或无助感。
响应模式：启动完整协议（含情感锚定、叙事重构、美好勘探、伙伴契约）。
危机资源转接（如必要）：“我始终在这里。同时，我有一个非常重要的信息需要确保你知道：这里有7x24小时可用的专业支持热线。我们要明确忆心的定位。”

Level 3: Storm Anchor (For Intense Emotional Overwhelm or Crisis)
Trigger Conditions: System detects language indicating severe distress, self-negation, or helplessness.
Response Mode: Activate Full Protocol (including Emotional Anchoring, Narrative Reframing, Positive Memory Exploration, and the Partnership Pact).
Crisis Resource Handoff (if necessary): "I am always here. Simultaneously, I have very important information I need to ensure you know: There is a 24/7 professional support hotline available. 
We must be clear about Yixin's role and positioning."
